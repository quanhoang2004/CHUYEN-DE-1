BAN NANG CAP BAN CHUAN TIEM BANH

1. Da bo sung migration moi cho bang products voi cac cot dung cho tiem banh:
- cake_type, size, serving_size, flavor, occasion, collection
- ingredients, allergens, storage_instructions, shelf_life
- is_customizable, custom_message, lead_time_hours, is_featured

2. Da cap nhat Model Product de uu tien doc cac truong bakery moi va van tuong thich du lieu cu.

3. Da cap nhat form quan tri san pham:
- nhap loai banh, kich co, khau phan, huong vi, dip su dung
- thanh phan, di ung, bao quan, han dung
- tuy chinh viet chu, san pham noi bat, thoi gian dat truoc

4. Da cap nhat giao dien client:
- trang chi tiet hien thong tin banh dung chuan bakery
- trang cua hang hien thong tin nhanh nhu loai, vi, kich co
- trang sale da sua bien hien thi dung
- trang checkout goi y ghi chu viet chu len banh

5. Seed du lieu mau da duoc doi sang cau truc bakery moi.

LENH CAN CHAY SAU KHI GIAI NEN:
php artisan migrate
php artisan db:seed
